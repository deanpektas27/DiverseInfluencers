WORKSHOP SPEAKER

PURPOSE

Workshop speakers are an integral part of our program.
As a speaker, you will conduct a workshop to establish a foundational knowledge of soft/hard skills for our Fellows. By volunteering your time, you will be helping us provide for our Fellows.

QUALIFICATIONS

    • Working professional in the accounting/consulting/finance/tech (minimum of 1 year experience required)
    • In-depth knowledge of specific workshop topics provided to you
    • Availability on Thursday evenings

BENEFITS

    • Practice your presentation skills by conducting a workshop for our Fellows
    • Network with other professional volunteers at workshops to grow your network

MENTOR

PURPOSE

At Diverse Influencers, we strongly believe in the value of mentorship. As a Mentor, you will have the opportunity to help an under-resourced CUNY freshman/sophomore with their professional development in the early stages of their career.

QUALIFICATIONS

    • Working professional in the accounting/consulting/finance/tech (minimum of 1 year experience required)
    • Availability to meet in person or have call with mentee once per month throughout duration of program
    • Leadership roles held on and off campus during time in college (Preferred)

BENEFITS

    • Create a long-lasting professional relationship with your mentee as they begin their professional career
    • Build your network by attending exclusive social events with all mentor/mentee pairings
    • Receive proper recognition for your impactful commitment on our bi-monthly newsletter
