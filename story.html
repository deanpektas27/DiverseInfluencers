OUR STORY

Since being founded in 2018, Diverse Influencers has been providing career coaching and support for CUNY freshmen and sophomores. By solely focusing on CUNY students, Diverse Influencers not only provides students with vital professional development, but provides companies a pool of young, diverse talent.
