OUR STORY

After two student leaders, Nick Guillen and Jasmine Nuñez, attending CUNY colleges noticed a lack of freshmen and sophomore career preparedness, they took action. Nick and Jasmine founded a nonprofit organization solely focused on the professional development of freshmen and sophomores. The two decided it would be best to focus on students attending the 18 CUNY colleges. By solely focusing on CUNY students, Diverse Influencers not only provides students with vital professional development, but provides companies a pool of young diverse talent.
